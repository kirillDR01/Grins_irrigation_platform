Age thresholds

● FRESH

≤ 3 days in stage. Healthy.

⚡ STALE 5d

4–7 days in stage. Time to follow up. Pending Approval uses 5-10d.

⚡ STUCK 12d

> 7 days (or >10d for pending). Auto-populates the "Needs Follow-Up" count.
