# Consulting Firm Cost Analysis: Grin's Irrigation Platform

**Analysis Date:** January 17, 2025  
**Purpose:** Estimate consulting firm costs to build the complete Grin's Irrigation Platform  
**Scope:** Full-featured field service automation system with 6 dashboards and 393 requirements  

---

## Executive Summary

**Bottom Line:** A consulting firm would charge **$850,000 - $1,500,000** to build the complete Grin's Irrigation Platform, taking 12-18 months with a team of 8-12 specialists.

| Timeframe | 5 Years Ago (2020) | Today (2025) |
|-----------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Total Project Cost** | $650,000 - $1,200,000 | $850,000 - $1,500,000 |
| **Timeline** | 15-24 months | 12-18 months |
| **Team Size** | 10-15 people | 8-12 people |
| **Hourly Rates** | $125-200/hour | $150-250/hour |

**Key Factors Driving Cost:**
- 393 detailed requirements across 6 integrated dashboards
- Complex irrigation-specific business logic
- Real-time GPS tracking and route optimization
- Offline-first mobile PWA for field technicians
- AI chat agent with 24/7 customer service
- Advanced scheduling with constraint-based optimization
- Complete accounting and marketing automation
