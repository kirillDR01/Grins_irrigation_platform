## Executive Summary

### What is Housecall Pro?

Housecall Pro is a cloud-based field service management (FSM) software designed for home service businesses including HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, irrigation, cleaning, and similar trades. Founded in 2013, headquartered in San Diego, California, with 45,000+ business customers.

### Key Business Model Points

- **Pricing Model:** SaaS subscription (monthly or annual billing)
- **Contract Terms:** No long-term contracts required
- **Free Trial:** 14-day free trial (MAX plan features, no credit card required)
- **Target Market:** Solo operators to mid-size service businesses (1-100+ employees)
- **Primary Value Proposition:** All-in-one platform replacing multiple disconnected tools

### Pricing Summary (Annual Billing)

| Tier | Monthly Cost | Users Included | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------------|----------|
| Basic | $59/mo | 1 user | Solo operators |
| Essentials | $149/mo | Up to 5 users | Small teams |
| MAX | $299/mo | Up to 8 users* | Scaling businesses |

*Additional users on MAX: $35/month each

## Detailed Tier Comparison

### Basic Plan - $59/month (annual) | $79/month (monthly)

**Target User:** Solo operators, owner-operators, businesses just starting out

**User Limit:** 1 user only

**Core Capabilities:**
- Scheduling and dispatching
- Quotes and proposals
- Invoices and payments
- Online booking
- Basic review management
- Job cost tracking
- Basic price book
- Customer communication
- Mobile app access (iOS & Android)
- Live phone and chat support

**Key Limitations:**
- No QuickBooks integration
- No employee GPS tracking
- No equipment tracking
- No advanced reporting
- No email marketing/postcards
- No visual price book
- No checklists
- Single user only

---

### Essentials Plan - $149/month (annual) | $189/month (monthly)

**Target User:** Small teams (2-5 employees), growing service businesses

**User Limit:** Up to 5 users

**Includes Everything in Basic, Plus:**
- QuickBooks Online and Desktop integration
- Postcards and email marketing
- Customer equipment tracking
- Premium review management
- Visual price book (with images, categories, subcategories)
- Employee GPS tracking (mobile phone-based)
- Custom checklists

**Key Differentiators from Basic:**
- Multi-user capability (up to 5)
- Accounting integration (QuickBooks)
- Marketing automation tools
- Field team visibility (GPS)
- Equipment history tracking

**Key Limitations:**
- No advanced custom reporting
- No dedicated onboarding specialist
- No escalated phone support
- No API/Zapier access
- Add-ons cost extra ($40/each for Sales Proposal Tool, Recurring Service Plans)

---

### MAX Plan - $299/month (annual) | $329/month (monthly)

**Target User:** Scaling businesses, larger teams (6-100+ employees)

**User Limit:** Up to 8 users included, additional users at $35/month each

**Includes Everything in Essentials, Plus:**
- Advanced custom reporting
- Dedicated onboarding specialist
- Data migration support
- Escalated phone support
- Zapier automations
- Open API access
- **FREE Add-Ons Included:**
  - Sales Proposal Tool (normally $40/mo)
  - Recurring Service Plans (normally $40/mo)

**Key Differentiators from Essentials:**
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- White-glove onboarding
- Priority support access
- Integration flexibility (API, Zapier)
- Built-in add-ons at no extra cost
- Scalable user capacity

### Known Limitations (from user reviews and analysis)

**Scheduling:**
- No built-in route optimization
- No automatic smart scheduling
- Limited drag-and-drop on mobile
- No multi-day job view

**Invoicing:**
- Limited invoice customization
- Multi-segment invoices create reporting complexity
- Job costing not integrated with QB invoices

**Reporting:**
- Basic and Essentials have limited reporting
- Custom reports only on MAX
- Some users outgrow reporting capabilities

**Mobile App:**
- Feature parity issues (mobile vs. desktop)
- Occasional sync/bug issues reported
- Some interfaces not mobile-optimized

**Support:**
- Recent shift to AI-first support (2025)
- Some users report difficulty reaching humans
- Support quality varies by plan level

**Pricing:**
- Add-on costs can accumulate quickly
- $110+ jump from Basic to Essentials for 2nd user
- Essential features locked behind higher tiers

**Missing Features:**
- No inventory management
- No built-in route optimization
- Limited job costing capabilities
- No native time-to-payroll conversion
